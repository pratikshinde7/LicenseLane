Learner's License (LL) Process

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age of 16 for non-geared motorcycles (under 50cc).
  • Minimum age of 18 for light motor vehicles (cars).
  • Must have parental consent if under 18.
  • Must be aware of traffic rules and regulations.

Documents Required

  • Proof of Age: Birth Certificate, Passport, or PAN Card.
  • Proof of Address: Aadhar Card, Voter ID, or Utility Bill.
  • Application Form: Form No. 2, filled out completely.
  • Photographs: Recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Medical Certificate: Form 1 and 1A, if applicable.

Application Process

  1. Visit the official Parivahan Sewa website.
  2. Fill out the online application form for a Learner's License.
  3. Upload scanned copies of all required documents.
  4. Book a slot for the Learner's License test online.
  5. Pay the required fees online.
  6. Appear for the test at the designated RTO on your chosen date. The test is usually a computer-based quiz on traffic rules.
  7. Upon passing the test, your Learner's License will be issued.
