Permanent Driving License (DL) Process

Eligibility Criteria

  • You must hold a valid Learner's License (LL).
  • You can apply for a Permanent License after 30 days and within 180 days of your LL being issued.
  • You must be proficient in driving the class of vehicle for which you are applying.

Documents Required

  • Valid Learner's License: Your original LL is mandatory.
  • Application Form: Form No. 4.
  • Vehicle Documents: Valid Registration Certificate (RC), Insurance Certificate, and Pollution Under Control (PUC) certificate for the vehicle you will use for the test.
  • Photographs: Recent passport-sized photographs.

Application Process

  1. Visit the official Parivahan Sewa website.
  2. Fill out the online application form for a new Driving License.
  3. Book a slot for your practical driving test online.
  4. Pay the required fees for the test and the smart card license.
  5. Appear for the practical driving test at the RTO on your scheduled date and time with your vehicle.
  6. An RTO inspector will assess your driving skills.
  7. Upon passing the test, your Permanent Driving License will be dispatched to your registered address.
